About Arati Precision Industries

ARATI PRECISION INDUSTRIES is a premier mechanical manufacturing company committed to delivering precision-engineered components that meet the most demanding global standards.

Founded on the principles of accuracy, durability, and innovation, we specialize in high-precision mechanical parts used across automotive, steel, power, defense, and industrial machinery sectors.

Our facility is equipped with advanced CNC machining centers, surface grinders, and precision measuring instruments including Mitutoyo gauges, ensuring every component meets exact specifications down to micron-level tolerances.

Our Commitment

Quality Assurance

Quality is not just a process at Arati Precision — it is our culture. Every component undergoes multi-stage inspection before it leaves our facility.

01

Raw Material Inspection

Every batch of raw material is verified for chemical composition, hardness, and dimensional accuracy before production begins.

02

In-Process Quality Checks

Dimensional checks at every machining stage using calibrated precision instruments to detect deviations early.

03

Hardness Testing

Vickers, Brinell, and Rockwell hardness testing ensures heat-treated components meet specified hardness values precisely.

04

Final Precision Measurement

Final inspection using Mitutoyo micrometers, vernier calipers, height gauges, CMM, and surface roughness testers.
